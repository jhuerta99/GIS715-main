Brazil Wind Map

This dataset contains information on the Brazil wind. The glyphs are depicted as arrows, which fits nicely as wind direction. The smaller the arrow, the smaller the wind max is. I used a light blue to show lower temperatures and a dark blue to depict the higher temperatures. The map shows how there are many changes to wind direction, max temperature, and temperature in this region well. The data is from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/PROPPG-PPG/hourly-weather-surface-brazil-southeast-region?resource=download

Happiness Index in the Middle Ease depicted with pie charts

This layout contains the middle eastern countries of Saudi Arabia, Yemen, and Oman and their happiness indexes displayed in a pie chart. The happiness indexes included in the pie charts are the percentage of corruption, the freedom to make life choices, social support, GDP, healthy life expectancy, and generosity. The happiness index is included by the size of the pie charts; larger is more happy and smaller is less happy. This map conveys that there is low generosity in these middle eastern countries well. Data source: http://worldhappiness.report/ed/2018/

Happiness Index in the Middle East

This layout contains three happiness indexes. They are social support, freedom to make life choices, and perception of corruption. The data is from the Gallup World Poll. Data source: http://worldhappiness.report/ed/2018/
