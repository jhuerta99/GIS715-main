Lab 4: Kepler.gl

This section includes a visualization on kepler.gl about the crime in Raleigh from 2014 to 2020. It depicts the type of crime as various shades of color. If the legend doesn't pop up right away, click on the legend button in the top righthand corner. The size of the dots correlate to the amount of that type of crime reported in that area on that date. Data source: https://data-ral.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/raleigh-police-incidents-nibrs
