Lab 6: Part two to Tableau

Tableau Border Crossing Inbound Map for 2016

This map was made from the Bureau of Transportation Statistisc Border Crossing data for 2016. It shows summary statistics for inbound crossings at the US-Canada border and the US-Mexico border. The border crossing data is collected from ports of entry with transportation sources that include vehicles, flights, passengers, and pedestrians that enter into the United States.

Chart of Highest Inbound Crossings in 2016

This chart was made in tableau and shows the highest states that had inound crossings in 2016. The size of the rectangle correlates with the amount of border crossings in each state. The color also correlates with the amount of crossings; dark means more and lighter means less. The chart also only shows the top seven states with crossings in 2016.

Bar chart Showing Amount of Crossings in 2016 per Border State

This bar chart shows the amount of crossings per border state in 2016.

Bubble Chart of Inbound Crossings in 2016

This bubble map shows the highest inbound crossings per border state in 2016 with the size of the bubbles. The larger bubbles pop out at you more because they are larger and have more inbound crossings. The colors are randomized in this chart.
